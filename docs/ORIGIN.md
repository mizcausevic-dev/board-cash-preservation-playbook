# Origin

Board Cash Preservation Playbook exists after the scorecards, trust-room, procurement-answer, RFP assembly, proof-gap, board memo, benchmark, scenario, allocation, and priority layers.

The earlier tools show evidence, narrative, benchmark, allocation posture, and board priority. This repo turns that stack into one preservation surface so leadership can explain what to freeze, what to ring-fence, what to defer, what to hold, and which next move survives a board or diligence room.
